Overview

A people management platform designed to empower individuals to visualize, track, and advance their career growth. At its core are 3D skill trees (more like Clusters), personalized to each user, showcasing their skills, education, and career milestones in an intuitive and interactive format.

Dynamic 3D Skill Tree visualization

Features

  • Interactive 3D skill trees powered by AI and 3D modeling.
  • Personalized career recommendations for skill acquisition and development.
  • Gamified professional growth with "level up" features.
  • Seamless exploration of job opportunities aligned with user aspirations.

Gamified career advancement visualization

How It Works

SkillSphereAI integrates job market insights, education tools, and real-time progress tracking to create an engaging, structured, and rewarding career development journey. Users can visualize their current position, set goals, and follow recommended pathways to achieve them.
